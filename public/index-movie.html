This is my list of favourite Woody Allen movies:

1. Midnight in Paris

What’s not to like about Paris, time travel, Hemingway, Picasso, and the Lost Generation

2. Manhattan

Worth watching just for the opening sequence of fireworks set to Gershwin’s music and Mariel Hemingway

3. Play it Again Sam

A film adaptation of a hit Broadway play with Woody, Diane Keaton, and the ghost of Humphrey Bogart
